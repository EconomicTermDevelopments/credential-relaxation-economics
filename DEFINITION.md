# credential relaxation: Technical Definition

    ## Formal Definition
    Credential relaxation describes the process by which employers reduce or eliminate formal educational and certification requirements for job positions in response to labor shortages, shifting toward skills-based assessment. This phenomenon represents a reversal of credential inflation, where positions that previously required bachelor's degrees or specific certifications are opened to candidates demonstrating equivalent competencies through alternative pathways.
